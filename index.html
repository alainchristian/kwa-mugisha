Turi hano kubafasha

Duhagurukira kubahiriza abakiriya bacu ibicuruzwa byiza, bishya kandi ku giciro cyiza. Turi ahantu heza i Kimironko kwa Mushimire, Kigali. Turi kumwe nawe buri munsi, tuzanire umuryango wawe ibikenerwa byose.

Kuki Utore Kwa Mugisha?

Ibiciro byo hasi

Tuguhiriza ibicuruzwa byiza ku giciro gihagije. Twizeye ko amafaranga yawe agomba kugira agaciro.

Ibicuruzwa bishya

Imbuto n'imboga bishya buri munsi. Tugura ibintu byiza kandi bishya ku baguzi bacu.

Abakozi bacu bakunezereye kandi bakubana neza. Uri umuryango wacu!
